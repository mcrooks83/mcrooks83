## 🚀 Nexus N3

I lead the development of **Nexus N3**, an open and extensible engineering toolchain for building complete sensor-driven research and operational systems.

Nexus N3 brings together sensors, embedded hardware, communications, edge computing, AI, algorithms, analytics and user-facing workflows. It supports systems that need to operate locally, reliably and independently of continuous cloud connectivity.
